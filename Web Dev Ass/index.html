Requirements for New Students

A prospective applicant must be a high school graduate who has not officially enrolled in any post secondary course in any school. He/she should present the following:


  • Official Transcript of Record
  • Honorable Dismissal
  • Photocopy PSA Birth Certificate
  • 2pcs 2x2 picture
  • Long white folder
  • Long white plastic envelope
